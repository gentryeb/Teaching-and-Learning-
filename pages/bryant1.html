Digital Yoknapatawpha Escape Room for As I Lay Dying

Description:

Complete a digital escape room using various DY tools and reflect on information acquired

Modality:

In-Person

Lesson Plan

Preface

This activity is meant to help orient your students to “where in the world” they are as they read. This lesson is to be used before reading the novel and highlights the skill of aesthetic impact (through the cultural perspective/setting) and author’s structural choices (through the narrative structure). The digital escape room became popular in the age of COVID, but this lesson is adaptable for a physical escape room, as well. If you are unfamiliar with digital escape rooms, please check out the Resources section for more information!

Activities

Activity: Escape Room

Explore
Instructions

Create Groups

Break students into groups of three and have each student take on a role: researcher, technician, recorder.

  • The researcher is in charge of maneuvering the group through the DY website
  • The technician keeps the Escape Room form up and inputs the codes/responses
  • The recorder keeps track of all the answers and locations of answers in one document so they can be referenced later

Customize Google Form

Students work through the escape room Google Form. This link generates a copy that can be customized to suit the particular class context.

Tip: Students will need a personal device (cellphones are not recommended for the purpose of navigating the DY website, but can be used for the Google Form or record keeping)

Respond: Discussion Questions
Please write a response to the following questions:
  • What do you find out?
  • Compare your results if you change Gender to Male, or Race to Black
  • A third question about the little girl in the picture.
